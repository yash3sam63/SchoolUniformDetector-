About School Uniform Detector

The School Uniform Detector is a powerful AI-based web application designed to automatically identify school uniforms in images. Built using state-of-the-art machine learning models, this tool simplifies the process of determining whether students are wearing uniforms by analyzing uploaded photos.

With an easy-to-use interface, users can upload images for quick detection. Whether it's for ensuring compliance, automating monitoring in educational institutions, or simply conducting research, the School Uniform Detector offers accurate and efficient results. Our system is built on advanced image recognition technology, ensuring that every uniform pattern, color, and style is detected reliably.
